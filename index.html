Candidate Feedback Analysis

AI Recruiter (Alex) Performance Dashboard

Executive Summary

We recently deployed an AI-powered recruiter, Alex, to conduct initial candidate screening and interviews. To assess the effectiveness of this capability and benchmark the candidate experience, we collected structured feedback from all interviewed candidates. This dashboard analyzes 2,347 candidate responses, delivering a data-driven view of what is performing well and where operational improvements are required. Post-interview, candidates submitted structured feedback capturing satisfaction and experience quality. These responses were consolidated, normalized, and classified into Positive, Neutral, and Negative sentiment buckets. In parallel, qualitative feedback was systematically analyzed to identify recurring experience themes, enabling both quantitative trend analysis and deeper qualitative insight generation. The outcome is a unified performance view that supports informed decision-making, continuous optimization, and scalable improvement of the AI recruiting workflow.

Key Finding: 88% of candidates had a positive experience with the AI recruiter, with an average satisfaction score of 4.47/5. However, most of the negative reviews come from candidates expressing a strong preference for human interaction over AI.

What We're Measuring

  • Rating Distribution: How candidates scored their experience (1-5 scale)
  • Sentiment Analysis: Overall positive, neutral, or negative feedback
  • Theme Identification: Common topics mentioned in feedback

Objectives

  • Evaluate the effectiveness of AI-assisted recruiting
  • Identify technical issues impacting candidate experience
  • Guide improvements to our recruitment technology

What to Look For

As you explore this dashboard, pay attention to:

  • Rating trends: Notice the heavy concentration in 4-5 star ratings
  • Theme patterns: "AI vs human" appears frequently in negative feedback
  • Conversation flow: Interruptions are a common complaint

Conclusions & Next Steps

What's Working Well

  • High overall satisfaction (88% positive, 4.47/5 average)
  • Strong performance on ease of use and human-like interaction
  • Effective question quality and professionalism

Areas for Improvement

  • Set expectations: Add disclaimer that interview will be AI-assisted
  • Fix timing issues: Reduce interruptions and improve conversation flow detection
  • Technical stability: Address audio/connection quality issues
  • Q&A capability: Allow candidates to ask more questions about the role
