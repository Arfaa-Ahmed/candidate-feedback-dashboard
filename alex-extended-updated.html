Alex AI Recruiter – Top of Funnel Analysis

Date Range analyzed • [2025-04-01 → 2026-02-01]

Executive Summary

This analysis reviews Alex AI Recruiter's Top-of-Funnel (TOF) performance over a 10 month period from April 2025 upto February 2026, covering 13,320 total interview invitations.

The platform delivered a 36.4% overall completion rate (4,843 interviews), reflecting sustained engagement at scale. Engagement depth is strong: 51.1% of invited candidates have progressed beyond the "Sent" status, while 48.9% remain pending, representing optimization upside in activation. Downstream efficiency is a clear strength: 71.1% of candidates who engage ultimately complete their interview, indicating a healthy interview experience and effective automation flow.

Operational impact is material 1,148 recruiter hours saved across 4,807 completed interviews translates to approximately 14.3 minutes saved per interview, equivalent to 143 recruiter workdays of capacity freed for higher-value activities.

The 6,510-invite "waiting" pool (49% of total) represents the highest-leverage optimization area, while 16.3% stall rate post-action indicates meaningful friction that warrants process improvements.
