Alex AI Recruiter – Top of Funnel Analysis

Date Range analyzed • [2025-11-10 → 2026-01-15]

Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates Top-of-Funnel (TOF) performance for Alex AI Recruiter's automated interview platform from approximately November 2025 through mid-January 2026, covering 5,839 total interview invitations.

The platform achieved an overall interview completion rate of 34.0% (1,986 completed interviews), representing a reasonable baseline level of candidate engagement for automated outreach, with meaningful opportunity for improvement as messaging, cadence, and targeting mature.

From an engagement perspective, 47.8% of invited candidates have taken action after receiving an invitation, progressing beyond the initial "Sent" state into scheduling, completion, or other downstream statuses. Conversely, 52.2% of invitations remain pending response, indicating the primary optimization opportunity lies in improving candidate activation and response rates.

Operational throughput remains highly efficient, averaging approximately 29 completed interviews per day at roughly 6 hours of daily system runtime, yielding approximately 4.8 interviews per hour. This materially exceeds sustainable human recruiter capacity and demonstrates tangible automation ROI.

Alex and Sense operate under fundamentally different engagement mechanics. Sense benefits from real-time voice engagement, while Alex relies on candidate-initiated response via SMS scheduling. Direct conversion comparison requires channel normalization.

Critical Findings & Recommendations

Post-Contact Conversion
Finding: Alex achieved a 34.0% overall interview completion rate (1,986 completed out of 5,839 invitations). This represents a reasonable baseline for automated outreach, but indicates meaningful headroom for improvement as activation strategies mature.
Primary Bottleneck: Candidate Response Activation
Finding: 52.2% of invitations remain pending response in the “Sent” state, while 47.8% of invited candidates have taken action. The largest opportunity is improving candidate response activation through messaging quality, cadence, and channel effectiveness
Proven Automation ROI
Finding: Alex delivers approximately 4.8 completed interviews per hour, materially exceeding sustainable human recruiter throughput and demonstrating clear automation-driven productivity and ROI.
Mid-Funnel Friction Opportunity
Finding: Approximately 803 candidates (~13.8% of total invitations, or ~28.8% of engaged candidates) fall into non-completion outcomes (Scheduled, Paused, Incomplete, Missed). This represents a secondary optimization opportunity to reduce drop-off once candidates engage.
Positive Candidate Experience Signal
Finding: Candidate feedback ratings skew strongly toward 4–5 stars with minimal 1–2 star detractors, indicating positive perceived experience. While sample size and response bias should be considered, this provides a supportive directional signal on interview quality.
