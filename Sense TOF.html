Sense AI Recruiter – Top of Funnel Analysis

Date Range analyzed • [2025-11-10 → 2026-01-15]

Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates Top-of-Funnel (TOF) performance for Sense AI Recruiter post-application voice flows between November 10, 2025 and January 15, 2026. All candidate-level metrics are calculated after deduplication using normalized phone number as the primary identifier, with name-based fallback where phone data is unavailable.

Across 4,964 call attempts representing 3,215 unique candidates, 62.4% of candidates successfully connected with the AI recruiter and 48.1% ultimately completed the interaction. Once a candidate connects, completion performance remains strong at 77.2%, indicating that in-call experience quality is generally healthy.

The primary funnel leakage occurs before connection, driven largely by voicemail reach, no-answer outcomes, and dialing failures.

Findings

Timing Optimization Opportunity
Finding: Evening slots (19:00-21:00) significantly outperform midday (13:00-15:00)
High Outreach Waste Before Engagement
Finding: 37.6% of contacted candidates (1,210 out of 3,215) never connected, representing ~2,400 unproductive call attempts driven primarily by voicemail and no-answer outcomes.
Pre-Connect is the Real Bottleneck
Finding: 77.2% of connected candidates complete (good!), but only 62.4% connect in the first place
Rescheduling System Works Well
Finding: 19.2% of in-call drops are "terminated_rescheduled" (agent-driven), vs 80.8% true terminations (user-driven)
In-Call Experience Quality Is Strong Once Connected
Finding: 77.2% of connected candidates successfully complete the interaction, indicating that interview flow design, user experience, and AI interaction quality are performing well once engagement is achieved.
